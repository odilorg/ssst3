**Pillar 3: Food & Culinary Culture**
- Target: 15-20 posts

- Goal: Promote culinary tour, rank for recipe/food keywords

**Pillar 4: Photography Guides**
- Target: 15-20 posts

**Pillar 7: Adventure Travel**
- Target: 10-12 posts

**Pillar 8: Tour Comparisons & Reviews**
- Target: 8-10 posts
- Topics: Operator comparisons, tour type comparisons, reviews

**Total Target:** 100-120 posts in first 6 months

### **Content Format Mix:**

**60% Educational/Guide Posts:**
- "How to..."
- "Complete Guide to..."
- "Step-by-Step..."
- "Ultimate Guide..."
- Length: 2,000-4,000 words
- Goal: Rank, build authority, educate

**20% Comparison/Review Posts:**
- "Best X for Y"
- "X vs Y Comparison"
- "Review of..."
- Length: 1,500-2,500 words
- Goal: Commercial intent, conversions

**10% Personal Experience Posts:**
- "My Experience..."
- "What I Learned..."
- "Behind the Scenes..."
- Length: 1,000-2,000 words
- Goal: Build trust, personality, engagement

**10% Update/News Posts:**
- "2025 Update: Uzbekistan Visa Changes"
- "What's New in Uzbekistan Tourism"
- Length: 500-1,000 words
- Goal: Freshness, show we're current

### **Content Length Strategy:**

**Skyscraper Posts (5,000-8,000 words):**
- "Complete Uzbekistan Travel Guide 2025: Everything You Need to Know"
- "Visiting Uzbekistan: 101 Questions Answered"
- "Photography Guide to Uzbekistan: 50+ Locations with Camera Settings"
- Goal: Rank for competitive keywords, definitive resource

**Pillar Posts (3,000-5,000 words):**
- "Samarkand Travel Guide: Registan, History, Hotels, Itineraries"
- "Khorezm Desert Fortresses: Complete Archaeological Guide"
- "Uzbekistan Culinary Tour: Complete Food Guide"
- Goal: Rank for medium-competition keywords, comprehensive

**Standard Posts (1,500-3,000 words):**
- Most blog posts
- Detailed but focused
- Goal: Rank for specific long-tail keywords

**Quick Answer Posts (1,000-1,500 words):**
- Specific questions
- Fast answers
- Goal: Featured snippets, voice search

### **SEO Optimization Standards:**

**Every Post Must Include:**
- [ ] Target keyword in title (front-load if possible)
- [ ] Title 50-60 characters (for search results)
- [ ] Meta description 150-160 characters with keyword
- [ ] URL structure: /keyword-rich-slug/
- [ ] Keyword in first paragraph
- [ ] Keyword in H1, H2, H3 headers
- [ ] Related keywords naturally included (LSI keywords)
- [ ] At least 5-10 internal links to other blog posts and tour pages
- [ ] At least 2-3 external links to authoritative sources (.edu, .gov, Wikipedia)
- [ ] Alt text for all images (keyword where relevant)
- [ ] Image file names: descriptive-keyword.jpg (not IMG_1234.jpg)
- [ ] Mobile-optimized (responsive design)
- [ ] Fast loading (compressed images, good hosting)
- [ ] Schema markup (Article, FAQ, Review, HowTo where applicable)

**Content Quality Standards:**
- [ ] Original content (no copying competitors)
- [ ] Expert author byline (establish E-E-A-T: Experience, Expertise, Authority, Trust)
- [ ] First-hand experience emphasized
- [ ] Specific details (dates, names, prices, locations)
- [ ] High-quality original photos (10-20 per post minimum)
- [ ] Recent information (2025 dates, current prices, updated logistics)
- [ ] Clear structure (table of contents, headers, bullet points)
- [ ] Actionable information (not just fluff)
- [ ] Answer specific questions
- [ ] Include personal anecdotes/stories
- [ ] Add "Last updated" date (show freshness)
