## WHAT TO RESEARCH ABOUT ADVANTOUR

### **1. Content Volume**

**Questions to answer:**
- How many total Uzbekistan pages/posts?
- How many destinations covered?
- How many practical information pages?
- How many cultural/historical pages?
- How many actual blog posts (with dates)?

**Method:**
- Count pages in navigation
- Use site search: site:advantour.com/uzbekistan
- Check sitemap if available (/sitemap.xml)

### **2. Content Depth**

**Questions to answer:**
- Average word count per page?
- Are guides comprehensive or superficial?
- Do they include personal experiences?
- Do they have photos (original or stock)?
- Do they have videos?
- Do they have maps?

**Method:**
- Open 5-10 representative pages
- Copy text into word counter
- Scroll through - look at photo quality/originality
- Check for "last updated" dates

### **3. Content Freshness**

**Questions to answer:**
- When were pages last updated?
- Are there 2025 updates?
- How often do they publish new content?
- Is information current (prices, logistics, visa)?

**Method:**
- Look for "last updated" dates on pages
- Check news page dates
- See if 2025 information is included

### **4. SEO Strategy**

**Questions to answer:**
- Do pages have target keywords in titles?
- Are meta descriptions optimized?
- How's the URL structure?
- Internal linking strategy?
- Schema markup used?

**Method:**
- View page source (Ctrl+U)
- Look at <title> tag
- Look at meta description
- Check URL structure
- Right-click ‚Üí Inspect ‚Üí find schema markup

### **5. Conversion Strategy**

**Questions to answer:**
- How do they link to tours from content?
- Where are CTAs placed?
- What language do they use?
- Do they have lead magnets (email capture)?
- Do they have special offers?

**Method:**
- Click through from content to tour pages
- Note CTA buttons/links
- Check for popups, banners, etc.
