## ‚ö†Ô∏è RISKS & MITIGATION

### Risk 1: Photography Tour - Can't Find Pro Photographer Guide
**Mitigation:**
- Option A: Partner with Uzbek photographer (search wedding photographers who speak English)
- Option B: Bring guide from Europe/US (add $2-3K to tour cost, adjust pricing)
- Option C: Start with Classic tour first, add Photography later when guide found

### Risk 2: Culinary Tour - Can't Vet Cooking Partners
**Mitigation:**
- Start with 1-2 cooking classes (not 4-5)
- Use established restaurants (safer than home-hosted initially)
- Conduct video calls with potential partners
- Offer "culinary experiences" not full cooking school
- Travel to Uzbekistan to vet partners personally (ideal)

### Risk 3: Pricing Too High / Too Low
**Mitigation:**
- Start 10% below target ($180-200 instead of $200-220) for first bookings
- Get reviews first, then increase price
- Monitor competitor pricing changes
- Survey customers after tours ("What would you pay?")

### Risk 4: Can't Fill Tours
**Mitigation:**
- Launch with guaranteed departures (even 1 person)
- Build blog/content marketing 3 months before season
- Offer early-bird discounts (10-15% off)
- Target shoulder season first (easier to fill than peak)
- Partner with travel agents for distribution

### Risk 5: Competitors Copy Us
**Mitigation:**
- Focus on execution excellence (hard to copy relationships)
- Build brand loyalty early (community, email list)
- Continuous innovation (add new experiences yearly)
- Create moat: Photography guide network, home-hosted families, special partnerships
