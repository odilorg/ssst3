# Uzbekistan Tour Operator Competitive Research - Framework

## Research Objectives
1. Analyze 6-8 established Uzbekistan tour operators
2. Map their complete tour portfolios
3. Identify pricing benchmarks and patterns
4. Find gaps and underserved market segments
5. Develop differentiation strategy for our tours

## Competitors to Analyze (Official Licensed Operators)

### Tier 1: Major Players (Focus)
1. **Advantour** (www.advantour.com) - Market leader, 20+ years
2. **Kalpak Travel** (kalpak-travel.com) - Swiss-based, Central Asia specialist
3. **PeopleTravel** (www.samarkand-tours.com) - Major operator

### Tier 2: Established Operators
4. **Anur Tour** (www.anurtour.uz)
5. **Dolores Travel Services** (www.sambuh.com)
6. **Central Asia Travel** (www.centralasia-travel.com)

### Tier 3: Additional Research (if needed)
7. **Uzbek Tour** (uzbektour.uz) - Local Samarkand operator
8. **Pahlawan Travel** (www.pahlawan.uz)

## Data Points to Collect Per Operator

### 1. Company Overview
- Years in operation
- Headquarters location
- Market positioning (budget, mid-range, luxury, specialist)
- Target audience
- Unique selling proposition

### 2. Tour Portfolio
- Total number of tours offered
- By duration (3-5 days, 6-8 days, 9-12 days, 13+ days)
- By category (cultural, adventure, thematic, festival, etc.)
- By season (year-round, seasonal only)
- By type (group, private, custom)

### 3. Pricing Analysis
- Average price per day (by category)
- Price range for:
  - 7-day standard tours
  - 10-day comprehensive tours
  - 12+ day extended tours
- What's typically included vs. excluded
- Single supplement policies
- Seasonal pricing differences

### 4. Detailed Itinerary Analysis (Top 5 Tours)
For each major tour:
- Complete day-by-day structure
- Cities visited and order
- Transport methods
- Accommodation standards
- Meal inclusions
- Unique experiences/excursions
- Pacing (activities per day, free time)

### 5. Customer Review Themes
From TripAdvisor, Google, TourRadar:
- Common praise (what they do well)
- Common complaints (what to avoid)
- Suggestions/requests (market opportunities)

### 6. Gap Analysis
What they DON'T offer:
- Underserved destinations
- Underserved audiences (solo, families, young professionals, photographers, etc.)
- Seasonal gaps
- Price point gaps
- Tour type gaps

## Output Format

For each competitor, create: `competitor-research/[operator-name].md`

Then create synthesis: `competitor-research/market-analysis-summary.md`

## Success Metrics
- Analyze 30-50 tours across all operators
- Identify 10+ actionable gaps/opportunities
- Develop differentiation strategy for 10-15 tours
- Benchmark pricing for accurate competitive positioning
