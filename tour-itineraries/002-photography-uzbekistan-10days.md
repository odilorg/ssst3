## RECOMMENDED CAMERA EQUIPMENT

**Ideal Gear (Not Required, But Recommended):**
- DSLR or mirrorless camera (any brand)
- Wide-angle lens (16-35mm) for architecture
- Mid-range zoom (24-70mm) for versatility
- Telephoto lens (70-200mm) for portraits/details
- Sturdy tripod (essential for golden hour, blue hour, night shots)
- Extra batteries (3-4) - charging opportunities daily
- Extra memory cards (64GB+ each)
- Circular polarizing filter (blue skies, reduce reflections)
- ND filter for long exposures
- Lens cleaning kit (desert dust!)
- Laptop with Lightroom/Photoshop for workshops
- Comfortable camera bag (lots of walking)

**Minimum Viable Setup:**
- Any interchangeable lens camera OR high-end compact
- One versatile zoom lens (24-105mm equivalent)
- One extra battery
- One tripod (we can loan if needed)
- Smartphone with good camera (workable for many shots!)

**What Your Guide Will Bring:**
- Full-frame DSLR + lens kit (demonstration purposes)
- Tripods for loan
- Light reflector for portraits
- Laptop for workshops
- Sample images for teaching
- Shot lists for locations

- **Location:** Quiet spot outside Ichan-Kala walls or rooftop
- **Subject:** Stars over desert and historic monuments
- **Conditions needed:** Clear skies, minimal light pollution (Khiva has some but workable)
- **Techniques taught:**
  - Milky Way photography (if visible and season appropriate - best June-August)
  - Star trails (long exposures 15-30 minutes or stacked images)

- **Equipment:** Tripod absolutely essential, remote shutter release helpful
- **Settings:** ISO 1600-6400, aperture f/2.8-f/4, shutter 15-30 seconds, manual focus
- **Difficulty:** High - guide assists extensively
