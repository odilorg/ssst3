# Image Download Guide for Tours and Cities

## Recommended Stock Photo Websites

### Free Resources (with Attribution)
1. **Unsplash** (https://unsplash.com)
   - High-quality, professional photos
   - Free for commercial use
   - No attribution required (but appreciated)
   - Search: "Uzbekistan", "Samarkand", "Bukhara", "Khiva"

2. **Pexels** (https://pexels.com)
   - Free stock photos and videos
   - High resolution
   - Free for commercial use

3. **Pixabay** (https://pixabay.com)
   - Free images, vectors, and videos
   - Large selection
   - Easy to search

4. **Creative Commons Search** (https://search.creativecommons.org)
   - Find Creative Commons licensed images
   - Various licenses - check each image

### Paid Resources (High Quality)
1. **Shutterstock** (https://shutterstock.com)
   - Largest stock photo library
   - Professional quality
   - $29/month for standard license

2. **Getty Images** (https://gettyimages.com)
   - Premium quality
   - Editorial and commercial licenses
   - Higher price point

3. **Adobe Stock** (https://stock.adobe.com)
   - Integrated with Adobe Creative Suite
   - High-quality images
   - Subscription available

## Image Specifications

### General Requirements
- **Format**: WebP (preferred) or JPEG
- **Hero Images**: 1920x1080px (16:9 ratio)
- **Gallery Images**: 1600x1200px (4:3 ratio) or larger
- **File Size**: Under 500KB for web optimization
- **Quality**: High resolution for retina displays

### Technical Tips
1. **Compression**: Use tools like TinyPNG or ImageOptim
2. **Responsive**: Create multiple sizes if needed
3. **Alt Text**: Descriptive alt text for accessibility
4. **Watermarks**: Avoid unless licensed
5. **Consistency**: Same style/filter across tour gallery

## SEO Best Practices

### Filenames
- Use descriptive names: `samarkand-registan-square-sunset.jpg`
- Avoid: `IMG_1234.jpg`
- Include keywords: city name, landmark, time of day

### Alt Text Examples
```
- "Registan Square at sunset with three madrasahs illuminated"
- "Tourists hiking in Chimgan Mountains near Tashkent"
- "Kalyan Minaret in Bukhara's historic city center"
- "Family in traditional Uzbek costumes in Khiva"
- "Traditional plov cooking class in Samarkand"
```

## Licensing Notes

### Free Images
- Check license on each site
- Unsplash: Free, attribution not required
- Pexels: Free, attribution not required
- Creative Commons: May require attribution

### Paid Images
- Keep receipts/licensing proof
- Check commercial use rights
- Understand model/property releases
- Check geographic restrictions

### Recommended Action
1. Start with Unsplash and Pexels (free)
2. For unique shots, consider paid sources
3. Take your own photos when possible
4. Get written permission for portraits
5. Create a licensing spreadsheet for tracking
